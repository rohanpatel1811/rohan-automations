Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2025

This site (rohan.automations) is a free website audit tool. This policy explains what data is collected when you use it and how it is used.

What we collect

When you run an audit, the following information is logged:

  • The URL you submitted for auditing
  • The Lighthouse scores returned (Performance, SEO, Accessibility, Best Practices)
  • Approximate location derived from your IP address — country, region, and city (provided by Vercel's infrastructure; your IP address itself is never stored)
  • The date and time of the audit

We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily book a call via the calendar widget.

How we use this data

Audit logs are stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to the site owner. The data is used solely to understand how the tool is being used — which types of sites are being audited and from which regions — so we can improve the service. We do not sell, share, or publish this data.

Google PageSpeed Insights

Audits are powered by Google PageSpeed Insights (PSI) API v5. When you submit a URL, your browser sends that URL directly to Google's PSI API. Google's own Privacy Policy applies to that request. We do not control what Google does with the URL you submit.

Cookies & tracking

This site does not use cookies, analytics scripts, advertising trackers, or any third-party tracking technology. No data is shared with advertisers.

Booking widget

If you choose to book a call, the calendar widget is provided by a third-party scheduling service (Cal.com). Their own privacy policy governs any data you enter there.

Data retention

Audit logs are kept indefinitely in the private Google Sheet for internal analysis. You may request deletion of any entry by emailing rohanpatel0088@gmail.com with the URL and approximate time of your audit.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.
